# Spanish Latin America (ES-419) — Language-Specific QA Rules

**Account:** Diligent Corporation
**Language:** Spanish (ES-419)
**Region:** Latin America
**Register:** Formal (usted)
**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## Register

- Always use **usted** (formal) — never **tú** (informal) or **vos**
- Latin American Spanish only — European Spanish (es-ES) is not accepted for this account
- Flag any vosotros usage immediately — this is European Spanish and must be corrected

---

## Punctuation

- Inverted question mark **¿** required at the start of every question
- Inverted exclamation mark **¡** required at the start of every exclamation
- Do not omit these — they are mandatory in Spanish, not optional

---

## Latin American vs European Spanish

Flag these European Spanish patterns as errors:

| European (incorrect) | Latin American (correct) |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| vosotros | ustedes |
| ordenador | computadora / computador |
| móvil | celular / móvil |
| coger | tomar |
| conducir | manejar |

---

## Date and Number Formats

- Date format: `DD/MM/AAAA` (e.g. `04/04/2026`)
- Decimal separator: comma — `1,5` not `1.5`
- Thousands separator: period — `1.000` not `1,000`
